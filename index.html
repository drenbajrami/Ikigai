About Us

Our company was founded by Hikaru Fujimoto and Sakura Nakamura in 2019. Initially, we started as a backpack company, but due to our success in Japan, we have expanded our product line to include pants, tops, and accessories. Now, we ship to North America, Europe, and many other areas.
